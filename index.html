About

At Vision First Optic, we pride ourselves in our service. What ever your needs are we at Vision First will do our best to serve you. Many of our customers have wrote exemplary reviews of us and through travel websites like Trip Advisor, the word of our service and low prices have reached many others from all over the world.

Many have travelled to Penang just to have their glasses made here, and they too join our multitude of very satisfied customers. Feel free to read the reviews below and on Trip Advisor from locals and people who are from abroad!
